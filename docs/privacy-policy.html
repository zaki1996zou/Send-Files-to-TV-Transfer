Send Files to TV Transfer

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Send Files to TV Transfer (“we,” “our,” or “the App”) helps you select files on your device and share or cast them to supported TVs and devices. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App may access, how it is used, and your choices.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Overview

Send Files to TV Transfer is a utility app for file sharing, casting, and device discovery. We do not require you to create an account or sign in. Most data stays on your device. We do not operate servers that store the files you choose to transfer.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, the following types of information may be processed:

  • Files you select — Photos, videos, audio, PDFs, and documents you choose through the file picker or related tools.
  • Device and app settings — Preferences such as transfer history settings, stored locally on your device.
  • Local network information — Data needed to discover TVs and cast devices on your Wi‑Fi network (for example, Chromecast, DLNA, and AirPlay-compatible devices).
  • Transfer history — If enabled in Settings, recent transfer activity is saved locally on your device.
  • Network performance data — If you use the optional Network Speed Test, the App may download a small amount of test data from public test endpoints to estimate connection speed.
  • Advertising data — The App displays ads through third-party advertising partners. Those partners may collect device identifiers, IP address, ad interaction data, and similar information as described in Section 7.

We do not knowingly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you contact us directly.

3. How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • Let you select, share, cast, or stream files to supported devices.
  • Discover compatible TVs and cast receivers on your local network.
  • Store your preferences and optional local transfer history on your device.
  • Run optional utility features such as the network speed test.
  • Display and measure advertisements.
  • Improve app stability and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests you send to us.

4. Files and Media Access

The App accesses photos, videos, audio, PDFs, and documents only when you choose them (for example, by tapping “Choose File” or using a file tool). Files remain on your device unless you share, cast, or export them through iOS or supported transfer methods.

We do not upload your selected files to our servers. File transfer and casting occur between your device and the destination TV or app you select.

5. Local Network Access

To help you find and connect to TVs and cast devices, the App may scan your local Wi‑Fi network using standard discovery protocols (including Bonjour/mDNS services such as Google Cast, AirPlay, and related services).

Local network access is used only when you use casting, device discovery, or related features. The App does not access your local network in the background for unrelated purposes.

6. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following locally on your device:

  • Settings and preferences — Using iOS local storage (SharedPreferences).
  • Transfer history — If enabled, stored in a local file on your device. You can disable or clear this history in Settings at any time.

Uninstalling the App removes locally stored app data from your device.

7. Advertising

Send Files to TV Transfer is supported by advertising. We use third-party ad networks that may collect and use information to show and measure ads, including:

  • Google AdMob — Google Privacy Policy
  • Meta Audience Network (Facebook) — Meta Privacy Policy
  • AppLovin MAX — AppLovin Privacy Policy

Ad partners may use device identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS, where permitted), IP address, app usage related to ads, and diagnostic data. On iOS, you can limit ad tracking in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Ad configuration may be loaded from a remote source so ad settings can be updated without releasing a new app version. That request does not include your personal files.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may interact with third-party services when you use certain features, including:

  • TV and cast devices on your network (Chromecast-style casting, DLNA, AirPlay).
  • Public network speed test endpoints.
  • Advertising partners listed above.
  • The iOS system share sheet and other Apple system services.

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party devices, platforms, or services.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only in these situations:

  • With advertising partners to display and measure ads (see Section 7).
  • When you choose to share or cast files to another device or app.
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the App, or others.

10. Children’s Privacy

Send Files to TV Transfer is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

11. Your Choices and Rights

You can:

  • Deny or revoke photo, file, or local network permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Turn off transfer history or clear it in the App’s Settings.
  • Limit ad tracking through your device’s privacy settings.
  • Stop using the App and uninstall it at any time.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights regarding access, correction, or deletion of personal information. To exercise those rights, contact us using the email below.

12. Data Retention

Files you select are not retained by us on our servers. Local app data (settings and optional transfer history) remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall the App. Advertising partners may retain data according to their own policies.

13. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for using secure networks when transferring sensitive files and for only sharing content you have the right to use.

14. International Users

The App may be used in different countries. Third-party ad and analytics partners may process data in countries other than your own. By using the App, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in those locations.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.
